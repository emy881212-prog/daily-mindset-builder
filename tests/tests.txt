# Tests
## Test 1 - Real garden reflection-to-harvest loop [sanity]
Viewport: mobile
Description: The supplied moonlit garden contains exactly four interactive beds and supports the core reward loop.
Steps:
1. Open Journal, enter at least 10 characters, save the reflection, and confirm the reward popup
2. Choose Plant in my garden, confirm four beds appear over the illustrated garden, then plant and water one carrot
3. Wait until the bed button says Harvest, select it once, then open Inventory
Expected: The supplied garden artwork is visible, all four beds are tappable, and Inventory shows at least 1 harvested carrot.
## Test 2 - Device-local Owner Preview and map navigation
Viewport: mobile
Description: The private Owner Preview unlocks all ten World Map destinations only in the active browser while every existing section remains reachable.
Steps:
1. Open the app with ?ownerPreview=enable and confirm the World Map opens with the Owner Preview banner
2. Confirm all ten destinations from Sunny Farm through Starlight Cove are unlocked, every label stays inside its card, and selecting Starlight Cove shows a preview success notice
3. Refresh, confirm Owner Preview remains active on this device, select Sunny Farm to open the unchanged Farm, then open Plans and select a demo plan
Expected: Owner Preview persists locally, all ten map destinations respond for testing, normal progress is unchanged, every mobile label stays inside its card, and demo Plans show no payment form.
## Test 3 - Local progress survives refresh
Viewport: desktop
Description: Saved progress remains available after a page reload.
Steps:
1. Save a journal reflection and a mood check-in
2. Plant and water one of the four illustrated garden beds
3. Refresh the page and revisit Journal, Mood, Farm, and Inventory
Expected: Journal, mood, resources, farm state, upgrades, and inventory persist locally after refresh.
