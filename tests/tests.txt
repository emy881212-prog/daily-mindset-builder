Viewport: mobile
Description: The supplied moonlit garden contains exactly four interactive beds and supports the core reward loop.
Steps:
1. Open Journal, enter at least 10 characters, save the reflection, and confirm the reward popup
2. Choose Plant in my garden, confirm four beds appear over the illustrated garden, then plant, water, and grow a carrot
3. Harvest the carrot and open Inventory
Expected: The supplied garden artwork is visible, all four beds are tappable, and the harvested carrot appears in Inventory.
