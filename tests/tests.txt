# Tests
## Test 1 - Reflection to harvest loop [sanity]
Viewport: mobile
Description: A user can complete the core reward and farming loop.
Steps:
1. Open Journal, enter at least 10 characters, and save the reflection
2. Open Farm, plant a carrot seed, water it, and wait until it is ready
3. Harvest the carrot and open Inventory
Expected: The reflection grants 3 seeds and 10 coins, and the harvested carrot appears in Inventory.
## Test 2 - Navigation and demo plans
Viewport: mobile
Description: Every requested section can be reached and subscription billing is clearly disabled.
Steps:
1. Use the bottom navigation and More menu to open each section
2. Open Plans
3. Select a demo plan
Expected: Every section opens, demonstration mode remains visible, and no payment form appears.
## Test 3 - Local progress survives refresh
Viewport: desktop
Description: Saved progress remains available after a page reload.
Steps:
1. Save a journal reflection and a mood check-in
2. Plant and water one crop
3. Refresh the page and revisit Journal, Mood, Farm, and Inventory
Expected: Journal, mood, resources, farm state, upgrades, and inventory persist locally after refresh.
