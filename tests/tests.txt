# Tests
## Test 1 - Real garden reflection-to-harvest loop [sanity]
Viewport: mobile
Description: The supplied moonlit garden contains exactly four interactive beds and supports the core reward loop.
Steps:
1. Open Journal, enter at least 10 characters, save the reflection, and confirm the reward popup
2. Choose Plant in my garden, confirm four beds appear over the illustrated garden, then plant and water one carrot
3. Wait until the bed button says Harvest, select it once, confirm the Farm dashboard carrot count increases to at least 1, then open Inventory and confirm the Harvested produce section appears
Expected: The supplied garden artwork is visible, all four beds are tappable, the Farm dashboard confirms at least 1 harvested carrot, and Inventory opens with its Harvested produce section.
## Test 2 - Device-local Owner Preview and map navigation
Viewport: mobile
Description: The private Owner Preview unlocks all ten World Map destinations only in the active browser while every existing section remains reachable.
Steps:
1. Open the app with ?ownerPreview=enable and confirm the World Map opens with the Owner Preview banner
2. Confirm all ten destinations are unlocked and every label stays inside its card; enter Crystal Falls, confirm its full location view, then choose Open my farm here and verify Farm shows Current farm location: Crystal Falls while the same four interactive garden beds remain
3. Refresh and confirm Crystal Falls is still the active Farm location, choose Change location, enter Forest Garden and open the Farm there, then confirm Forest Garden becomes the persisted Farm location and Plans remain demo-only
Expected: Owner Preview and the selected Farm location both persist locally, every unlocked map node opens a real destination, the chosen location appears inside Farm without changing its four beds or progress, return/change navigation works, and demo Plans show no payment form.
## Test 3 - Local progress survives refresh
Viewport: desktop
Description: Saved progress remains available after a page reload.
Steps:
1. Save a journal reflection and a mood check-in
2. Plant and water one of the four illustrated garden beds
3. Refresh the page and revisit Journal, Mood, Farm, and Inventory
Expected: Journal, mood, resources, farm state, upgrades, and inventory persist locally after refresh.
